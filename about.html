About Barangay Pansol

Barangay Pansol, located in Calamba City, Laguna, is widely recognized for its numerous private resorts, which have become popular destinations for both locals and tourists. Known for its hot spring resorts, Pansol offers a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of urban life, with an array of private pools and amenities designed for relaxation and recreation.

Our barangay prides itself on maintaining a welcoming atmosphere for visitors, while fostering a strong sense of community among residents. With its scenic backdrop of Mount Makiling, Pansol is an ideal place for family gatherings, company outings, and other social events.

We are committed to ensuring that our facilities meet the needs of our guests while preserving the natural beauty and cultural heritage of our community. Come visit us in Barangay Pansol, and experience the relaxation and tranquility of our private resorts.
